Industry and M&A analysis

A diversified company was planning to divest one of its business units. A consulting company was contracted to help and plan in the transaction.

TopCompetitors acted as a subcontractor to the consulting company to find and analyse potential buyer candidates and to collect data regarding recent industry aqcuisitions and mergers.

Industry and M&A analysis

The owner of this company approached retirement age, so it was time to find a buyer for the company. TopCompetitors was hired to do market and acquisition analysis identifying the largest and most potential buyer candidate companies in this market area.

The result of the analysis was a comprehensive market overview, a detailed overview on relevant mergers and acquisitions and a short-list of most potential buyer candidates.

Ongoing competitive and market intelligence

The customer is a software developer focusing on a global, vertical niche market. Their competitors include specialized software developers competing directly, and also large turnkey vendors being competitors and also potential partners for the company. Customers include both governmental organizations and private sector companies.

TopCompetitors provides ongoing competitive and market intelligence for the company management and provides quarterly reports to the company board. Quarterly reports include updated company and market facts, relevant news, acquisitions and regulatory and market changes. TopCompetitors also monitors relevant public tender and procurement sources for the company.

TopCompetitors Research

The customer company develops business process optimization software, which is used in many industries. Different industries have large number of competitors with simple applications, typically covering only part of the total customer needs.

TopCompetitors was contracted to research and find out the leading companies and applications in each industry area well as to analyze special requirements and "Best Practices" in each industry.

This analysis helped the customer to develop their own solutions and to better tailor and productize industry specific software solutions.
